Perlick Care - Account Deletion

Users of the Perlick Care mobile application can delete their account and associated personal data directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account

Follow the steps below to delete your account:

  1. Open the Perlick Care mobile application.
  2. Log in using your registered email address and password.
  3. Open the Profile Drawer from the main screen.
  4. Select the Unregister option.
  5. Confirm your account deletion request.

Once confirmed, your account and all associated personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems.

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • All personal data associated with your account will be permanently removed from our systems.
  • After deletion, you will no longer be able to access your account or related data.

Request Account Deletion Without Accessing the App

If you cannot access the Perlick Care app but would still like to delete your account, please contact our support team.

Email: help@perlick.com

Please include your registered email address in your request so we can locate and delete your account.
